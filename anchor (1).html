01 / SIGNAL

The sense no one talks about.

Interoception is how your body knows it exists — heartbeat, breath rhythm, muscle tension, the quiet thrum of being alive. Most people never notice it. For someone in a dissociative episode, it disappears entirely, and the world starts to feel like something happening at a distance.

◇ Observation

Existing tools talk to the thinking mind.

Grounding apps offer instructions to follow and prompts to think through — at the exact moment the thinking mind is the least reliable thing available.

Someone close to our team lives with derealization. Watching them navigate episodes revealed what every existing tool misses: this isn't a cognitive problem. It's a breakdown of the body's real-time sense of itself.

◇ Approach

Bypass the mind. Feed the body.

Anchor passively reads heart rate variability, breath cadence, and eye movement to detect when a dissociative shift is occurring — before the person has to say a word.

When the signal crosses a threshold, the world simply becomes more alive. Bioluminescent fish appear on nearby surfaces. A soft tone begins. No alarms, no clinical language. Just an ocean that arrives when you need it most.

Instead of asking users to think their way back to their body, Anchor gives the interoceptive system direct, sensory inputs to work with — disguised as an underwater world.
02 / PROTOCOL

Three paths back.

Each exercise targets a different channel of interoceptive awareness — spatial, tactile, respiratory — disguised as a task inside the glasses' underwater world.

◇ TASK_01[ SPATIAL_SEEK ]

Collect the seashells.

Anchor scatters AR shells across your real environment — on the table, the floor, the sill. Reaching out, turning your head, picking them up — every movement is a re-introduction between your body and the space around it.

◇ CHANNELPROPRIOCEPTION + VISION
◇ TASK_02[ VISUAL_TRACK ]

Snap the fish.

Bioluminescent fish swim through your view. Tracking them with your eyes, framing them, capturing them — it restarts the visual-seeking loop that grounds the nervous system in real space.

◇ CHANNELOCULAR_TRACKING
◇ TASK_03[ BREATH_SYNC ]

Breathe underwater.

The room submerges. Guided inhales expand with the current, exhales release bubbles upward. Restores the breath-heartbeat feedback loop — the most fundamental interoceptive signal there is.

Baking PawPaw

what's in your fridge today? ask Mr. Paw — he'll whisk up a recipe just for you.

A warm, hand-illustrated cooking game where your fridge becomes a recipe book. Pick your ingredients, chat with a tiny chef cat powered by Google's Gemini AI, and bake something cozy together. Built in Unity with a whole lot of love (and paw prints).

01

the AI recipe generator that knows what's in your fridge

Players collect ingredients into a virtual fridge, pick a difficulty (easy · medium · hard) and a time limit, and Mr. Paw reaches out to Google's Gemini API to cook up three tailored recipes they can actually make. The request goes through a Google Apps Script proxy so no API keys live in the game client.

built with · UnityWebRequest → Google Apps Script → Gemini API
02

chat with a tiny chef cat

A scroll-bottomed conversation window with a friendly cat persona. Press Enter and Mr. Paw replies in real time — kneading up answers "as warm and fluffy as fresh bread."

scripts · ChatManager.cs · TMP_InputField
03

the fridge & cabinet inventory system

A persistent FoodData singleton stores ingredients across Unity scenes using DontDestroyOnLoad. Each FoodButton carries its prefab, spawn position, and scale so the cabinet can reconstruct every chosen ingredient in a 3D-positioned layout. Click the fridge, grab what you need, walk to the kitchen — and it all shows up exactly where you left it.

tech · Singleton pattern · ScriptableObject-style data · SceneManager
04

snap your bakes

An in-game camera saves .pngs to persistentDataPath. The album auto-loads them as thumbnails in a grid.

05

achievements for cozy milestones

PlayerPrefs-backed badges trigger when you hit milestones like "made 5 recipes." A banner slides in with title, description, and a little chime.

06

painterly shader work

Custom ShaderLab/HLSL shaders give baked goods their glossy glaze and frosting drips. ~70% of the codebase is shader code.

07

URP scene flow

Seven Unity scenes stitched together — start, fridge, cabinet, kitchen, chat, recipe, camera — each with its own scripted transitions.

my ingredients in the mix

Baking PawPaw was a small-team hackathon project exploring whether an AI assistant could feel genuinely cozy rather than clinical — and we found the answer is yes, especially when it comes with paws.

I led the art direction and visual identity, designed the UI and scene flow, and implemented the integration between Unity and the Gemini API using a Google Apps Script endpoint to keep the build keyless and deployment-ready.

✓

Art direction & UI

Pastel palette, hand-painted textures, the cat-paw-fish wallpaper pattern, every button and frame.

✓

Game design & flow

Mapped the fridge → cabinet → kitchen → recipe loop; tuned the AI prompts for tone and recipe length.

✓

Character design

Designed Mr. Paw and his expressions — chef hat, whisker curls, little paint-brush paws and all.

✓

Gemini integration

Prompt engineering for difficulty/time-based recipes; wired the Unity client to a GAS proxy.
